FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does a full bike service take?

A full bike service typically takes 2-3 hours, depending on the condition of the bike.

Do you provide a warranty on repairs?

Yes, we offer a 30-day warranty on all repairs and parts.

Can I book a service online?

Absolutely! Use our Pre-Booking section to schedule your service.

What is the cost of services?

Service costs vary based on the bike model and service type. Please contact us or visit for a detailed quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, and online bank transfers.

Is there a guarantee on custom modifications?

Yes, we provide a 15-day guarantee on custom modifications for workmanship and parts.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before the appointment by contacting us at our support number.
